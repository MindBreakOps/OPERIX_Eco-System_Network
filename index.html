Core Infrastructure
Core Routing
Projects Workspace (The Hub)
The central command center where admins deploy new client projects and toggle active modules.
  • Supabase Infrastructure: Powered entirely by Supabase for lightning-fast, real-time data synchronization and bank-grade security—eliminating the latency of traditional setups.
  • Module Toggles: Choose exactly which boards are active for a specific client (e.g., enable Valet Ops but disable ANPR).
  • Project Types: Segregate projects by type (Operations, Entertainment, ANPR) to apply automated business logic.
  • Unified Dashboard: A single screen where managers can view all active corporate deployments and dive into specific boards.
Enterprise
Enterprise HRIS & Payroll
A massive standalone HR system handling map-based shift scheduling, automated payroll calculation, and recruitment.
  • Payroll Engine: Automatically calculates base salaries against GPS check-ins, dynamically applying deductions for late arrivals or absences. Generates printable payslips.
  • Map-Based Scheduling: Admins use integrated Leaflet/Nominatim maps to draw physical geofences for shifts, setting exact permitted GPS radiuses for staff.
  • Recruitment (ATS): Built-in Kanban pipeline for tracking job applicants from "Screening" to "Offered", complete with CV parsing.
  • Deep Profiles: Centralized storage for employee ID scans, driving licenses, and digital CVs.
Operational Modules
Field App
HR Field Board (ESS)
The mobile-first Employee Self-Service portal for frontline workers to interact with the Enterprise HRIS.
  • Geofenced Clock-In: Validates worker location against the site coordinates set by admins before allowing check-in.
  • Live Shift Tracker: Visual timer calculating earned salary, deductions, and late penalties dynamically in real-time.
  • Digital Requests: Employees can submit vacation/permission requests with attachments directly to admins.
Generators
Document Studio
Code-free generation of official business letters, job offers, contracts, and quotes with digital stamps.
  • Rich Text Editor: Draft documents directly in the browser with custom fonts (Cormorant, Space Grotesk, Amiri).
  • Digital Stamps: Configurable company seals and stamps applied directly to the document.
  • Export Engine: One-click conversion to high-resolution PDF or PNG.
  • Direct Sharing: Instantly share generated documents internally via the Mail Board.
Logistics
ANPR & Valet Operations
Automated Number Plate Recognition and end-to-end valet tracking with real-time digital ticketing.
  • Camera Integration: Live browser-based OCR scanning to detect and register license plates instantly.
  • Digital Invoices: Generates beautiful, print-ready parking tickets with QR codes.
  • Live Recall System: Valet screens flash and chime when a client requests their vehicle.
  • Financial Link: All paid parking operations sync directly to the Admin Accounting board.
Finance
Accounting Board
Admin-secured financial hub tracking project P&L, salaries, expenses, and asset valuation.
  • Project Segregation: View Net Profit vs Loss per specific operation (e.g., Mind Break Cafe).
  • Clearance Logs: Track outstanding supplier debts, client credits, and settled invoices.
  • Export Ready: Click to export financial views to Excel (XLSX) or CSV.
Comms
Internal Mail Board
Secure internal messaging system with custom HTML signatures and attachment handling.
  • Signature Builder: Generate rich-text, branded OPERIX signatures with employee photos.
  • Cloud Attachments: Seamlessly upload files to Google Drive and link them in messages.
  • External Bridge: "Open in Gmail" feature pre-formats the email and copies the HTML signature to clipboard for external client communication.
Hardware
Inventory & Assets
Physical hardware check-out system for operations equipment and cafe consumables.
  • Rapid Check-in/Out: Assign radios, keys, or tools to specific employees instantly.
  • Stock Alerts: Automated visual warnings when consumable items (e.g. Cafe Stock) fall below threshold.
  • Audit Trail: Every check-in/out logs the time, date, employee, and condition of the asset.
Support
Maintenance Ticketing
Two-sided application for staff to report issues and management to track repairs.
  • Smart Categorization: Route issues automatically (e.g., ANPR camera failure vs. Valet uniform issue).
  • Priority Triage: Color-coded visual badges for Normal, High, or Emergency level tickets.
  • Kanban & Table Views: Managers can drag and drop tickets from "Open" to "Resolved".
Infrastructure
Role-Based Security
Bank-grade security featuring 2-Factor Authentication, trusted devices, and admin passcodes.
  • Admin Passcode: A secondary 6-digit numeric pin required to access financial and system settings.
  • Trusted Devices: Fingerprints user browsers. Logging in from a new iPhone triggers an OTP challenge to a separate trusted email.
  • Module Locking: Frontline staff see only operations; Managers see HR; only Admins see Accounting.
